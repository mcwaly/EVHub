What You Need

Host Partner Requirements

We've kept it simple. If your property ticks these boxes, you're ready to partner.

✓  You Will Need

  • 🏢A physical location with parking spaces (minimum 2 dedicated EV bays)
  • ⚡Access to a 3-phase or 400V electrical supply at the site
  • 📶Reliable mobile or internet connectivity for station communication
  • 📄Valid business registration in Ghana or Nigeria
  • 🤝Willingness to sign a 3-year revenue-sharing partnership agreement
  • 📍Location visible and accessible to the public or target EV-driving audience

✗  You Do NOT Need

  • 🚫Any upfront capital investment — EVHub funds all hardware
  • 🚫Technical expertise — our engineers handle everything end-to-end
  • 🚫Your own payment processing infrastructure — we handle all transactions
  • 🚫Any prior experience with EV charging or clean energy
  • 🚫Staff dedicated to operating chargers — it's fully automated
  • 🚫Existing EV customers — we market the station and attract drivers

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying to become an EVHub partner.

Still have questions? Our partnership team is available Monday–Friday, 8am–6pm WAT, and would love to talk through your specific situation.

How much can I realistically earn as a Host Partner?▾
Earnings depend on your location traffic, number of charger ports, and local EV adoption. Our current host partners earn between ₵1,500 and ₵12,000 per month. High-footfall locations like malls and petrol stations typically earn at the higher end. Use our earnings estimator above for a personalised projection, and our team will provide a detailed forecast during the site assessment.
Who pays for the charger hardware and installation?▾
EVHub does. We fully fund the procurement, shipping, installation, and commissioning of all charging hardware. There is no upfront cost to become a Host Partner. In return, we share a percentage of revenue from each charging session conducted at your site.
What happens if a charger breaks down?▾
EVHub operates 24/7 remote monitoring on all stations. If a fault is detected, our system alerts our field engineering team automatically. We guarantee a response within 4 hours and resolution within 24 hours for all hardware faults. All repair costs are borne entirely by EVHub — you will never pay for maintenance.
How and when do partners get paid?▾
Revenue is calculated monthly and paid on the 5th of the following month. Payments are made via MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or direct bank transfer to your nominated account. You can track your accruing balance in real time through the Partner Dashboard on any device.
How long does onboarding and installation take?▾
From application submission to go-live, the typical timeline is 4–6 weeks. This includes site assessment (1 week), agreement signing (1–2 weeks), and hardware installation & commissioning (1–2 weeks). We move as fast as our partners need us to.
Can I apply if I'm not in Ghana or Nigeria yet?▾
Currently, our active programme is open to partners in Ghana and Nigeria. However, we are actively expanding and welcome interest from businesses in Senegal, Kenya, South Africa, and other African markets. Submit an application with your country noted and our expansion team will keep you updated on timelines for your region.
