FAQ

Quick Answers

Common questions before you reach out.

For urgent station faults, WhatsApp or our Live Chat are the fastest options — typical response under 15 minutes. You can also call our 24/7 emergency line for any safety-related issues at a charging station.
Open a chat or send an email to hello@evhub.africa with your session ID (shown in the EVHub app), phone number, and the amount charged. Our billing team will investigate and resolve or refund within 24 hours.
Select "Partnership" in the contact form above, or visit our dedicated Partner With Us page. For time-sensitive discussions, call our Accra office directly and ask to be connected to the partnerships team.
Yes. Send media enquiries, press releases, or interview requests to press@evhub.africa. Our communications team responds to press enquiries within 4 business hours. For urgent matters, call our Accra office and ask for the communications team.
Select "Investment" in the form above, or email invest@evhub.africa directly. Our investor relations team manages all funding discussions, tier enquiries, and due diligence requests. We respond to all investor enquiries within 1 business day.
Absolutely. Both our Accra and Lagos offices welcome visitors during business hours (Mon–Fri 8am–6pm). For specific meetings, we recommend booking in advance by calling or emailing your preferred office to ensure the right team member is available.
We're always looking for talented people who share our passion for Africa's electric future. Send your CV and a short note about why you'd like to join EVHub to careers@evhub.africa. We review all applications personally.
Our team is fluent in English, Twi, Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. We're working to expand language support as we grow across Africa. If you need support in a specific language, mention it in your message and we'll route you to the right team member.
