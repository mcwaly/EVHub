Our Origin

From Accra to Lagos — and Beyond

EVHub was founded in Accra, Ghana, by pan African entrepreneurs, and clean energy advocates who noticed a glaring gap: Africa was rapidly adopting electric vehicles, but charging infrastructure was nearly non-existent. Built on a longstanding commitment to innovation, infrastructure development, and inclusive growth. EVHub represents the convergence of purpose, technology, and execution.

Petrol stations had served Africa for over a century, but as Africa’s cities expand and transportation demand increases, the need for reliable, affordable, and built for local realities EV infrastructure — one that understood mobile money, load-shedding, and the spirit of community become critical. EVHub exists to enable this transition by developing and deploying dependable EV Charging solutions that support individuals, businesses, and institutions. Our approach prioritizes accessibility, operational reliability, and scalability; ensuring that clean transportation is practical, not hopeful.

We launched our first stations in Accra and Lagos, proving that world-class EV infrastructure was not just possible in Africa — it was inevitable. Today, our network is growing, and our ambition covers every one of the 54 nations on this great continent.

Our Core Values

Everything we build, every station we install, every driver we serve — guided by six unshakeable principles.

🌱

Sustainability First

Every decision we make prioritises the planet. From renewable-sourced power to minimal-waste hardware, green is in our DNA.

🤝

Community at Heart

We don't just build infrastructure — we build partnerships. Local jobs, local suppliers, and spaces that serve communities.

🔓

Radical Accessibility

Charging must be available to everyone. Our pricing, payment options, and locations are chosen with inclusivity in mind.

Africa's challenges are unique. We invent solutions that match — from solar-backed stations to offline-capable app modes.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

From a bold idea in a Accra co-working space to a growing pan-African network — here's how we got here.

2025 — Q1

The Spark: EVHub is Founded

An EV enthusiasts, and clean-energy advocates — incorporate EVHub in Accra, Ghana, with a mission to electrify Africa's mobility future.

Founding
2025 — Q3

Established Partnership with a leading player in Petroleum Industry in Africa

EVHub understand that building the nation’s green future doesn't happen overnight. It takes the patience to build lasting infrastructure, the goodwill to keep it affordable, and the faithfulness to ensure no community is left behind.

Milestone
2025 — Q4

Seed Funding & Expansion to Nigeria

EVHub closes a deal seed round led by pan-African climate investors. The Nigeira Lagos expansion kicksart, marking our cross-border expansion and the beginning of a West African network.

Funding
2026 — Q1

EVHub App Launches on Online

Our Website and mobile app — supporting real-time station tracking, mobile money payments, and session management — hits the App Store and Google Play. 5,000 downloads in the first week.

Product
2026 — Q2

20-Station Network Milestone

EVHub crosses 20 active charging points across Ghana and Nigeria, and announces the Partner Program — enabling businesses to host EVHub chargers and earn revenue.

Milestone
2026 — Now

Series A & Pan-African Roadmap

EVHub raises its Series A to accelerate expansion into Senegal, Kenya, and South Africa. Our vision: a charging station reachable by every African EV driver within 50km by 2030.
